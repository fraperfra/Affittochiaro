# Note per Designer e Sviluppatori

## 🎨 Palette Colori (Hex Codes)

**Primary (Green Branch)**
- Primary 500: `#00C48C` (Main Brand Color)
- Primary 600: `#00A376` (Hover)
- Primary 50: `#E6FFF5` (Backgrounds)

**Secondary (Teal Branch)**
- Teal 700: `#0A5E4D` (Owner Sections)
- Teal 50: `#E6F5F2` (Owner Backgrounds)

**Accent**
- Orange 500: `#FF6B35` (Call to Actions / Highlights)

**Text**
- Primary: `#2C3E50` (Headings)
- Secondary: `#64748B` (Body Text)

## ✍️ Typography

**Headings**
- Family: `Poppins`, sans-serif
- Weights: 600 (SemiBold), 700 (Bold)
- Tracking: Tight (-0.02em)

**Body**
- Family: `Inter`, sans-serif
- Weights: 400 (Regular), 500 (Medium)
- Line Height: 1.6 (Relaxed)

## 📐 Spacing & Grid

- **Container Max-Width**: `1280px` (standard desktop)
- **Grid System**: 12 columns
- **Border Radius**:
  - Cards: `16px` (1rem)
  - Buttons: `12px` (0.75rem)
  - Inputs: `12px` (0.75rem)

## 🎬 Interactions

- **Hover States**:
  - Buttons: Lighten/Darken by 10%
  - Cards: Translate Y -4px + Shadow Increase
- **Micro-animations**:
  - Fade In: 300ms ease-out
  - Scale Up: 200ms ease-out (used on badges/icons)

## 📱 Breakpoints

- **Mobile**: < 768px (Stack layouts, hidden navigation)
- **Tablet**: 768px - 1024px (2 column grids)
- **Desktop**: > 1024px (Full layouts)
